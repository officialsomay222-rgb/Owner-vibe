## 2024-05-16 - Adding aria-labels to MiniPlayer buttons\n**Learning:** Icon-only buttons in the MiniPlayer lack ARIA labels and tooltips, making them inaccessible to screen readers and slightly confusing for sighted users. While "Added ARIA label to button" is routine, the context of conditional `aria-label` changing based on `isPlaying` state is a notable UX pattern to apply to other media controls.\n**Action:** Add dynamic `aria-label` and `title` attributes that update based on the current playback state.
